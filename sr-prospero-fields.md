## PROSPERO Registration Instructions

1. Go to https://www.crd.york.ac.uk/prospero/
2. Click "Register a review"
3. Complete the form by copying from `sr-protocol.md` using the mapping above
4. Registration is **free**
5. You will receive a registration number (CRD4202xxxxxx) by email
6. **Important**: Registration must be completed BEFORE screening begins
7. PROSPERO allows updating the record later (e.g., when full search strings are finalized)
